# Maycation — Product Vision

---

## Mission

Give families back the time and mental energy they lose to coordination.

---

## Vision

A family vacation where the planning disappears and the experience remains.

---

## Who Maycation Serves

Families traveling together — parents, children, extended family, close friends traveling as a group — who share a trip and share the responsibility for it going well.

The person who manages the itinerary. The partner who tracks reservations. The kid who just wants to know what time they leave. The grandparent trying to keep up. Maycation is for all of them.

---

## The Problems Maycation Solves

**Planning is fragmented.**
Trips live across booking confirmation emails, group texts, shared notes, calendar invites, screenshot folders, and spreadsheets. No single view of the plan exists. Family members have different pieces.

**Coordination creates overhead.**
Someone has to maintain the master plan. That person carries a mental load the rest of the family doesn't see. Keeping everyone informed requires effort that shouldn't need to exist.

**Context switching is expensive.**
On a travel day, a family opens Maps, then their itinerary, then a booking email, then the weather app, then a messaging app to tell everyone the update. Every switch costs attention. Attention is limited.

**Information arrives too late.**
The reservation address is needed at check-in. The ticket time is needed at the gate. The day's plan is needed at breakfast. Maycation surfaces the right information before it becomes urgent.

---

## What Success Looks Like

A family reaches the start of their vacation — the airport, the hotel lobby, the theme park gate — without stress, without confusion, and without anyone spending the morning sending catch-up messages.

During the trip, everyone knows the plan. Changes reach everyone without a cascade of messages. The day unfolds the way it was meant to.

After the trip, the only thing the family remembers is the experience.

---

## What Maycation Is Not

**Not a booking engine.**
Maycation does not sell flights, hotels, restaurants, or tickets. It organizes what families have already planned.

**Not a travel guide.**
Maycation does not recommend where to go or what to do. Families bring their own decisions. Maycation holds them.

**Not a social network.**
Trips are private. Maycation is not for public sharing, follower counts, or travel content creation.

**Not a work tool.**
Maycation is designed for families and personal travel. It is not for business travel, project management, or team coordination.

**Not an expense tracker.**
Maycation is not a financial tool. Budget and expense management are out of scope.

**Not a competitor to the services families already use.**
Maps, weather, calendar, and reservations apps are best-in-class at what they do. Maycation integrates with them rather than replacing them.
